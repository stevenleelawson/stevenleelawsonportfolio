Who is this guy, exactly?

"Reformed musician turned developer and crime fighter"

After writing and recording music in various bands and getting my degree in Music, I started teaching myself the basics of coding in 2015. Wanting to learn more, I enrolled in the Turing Front End Development program where I really learned software development using React. Since then, I've enjoyed using my skills in ES6, React, and Redux to build software for a consultancy that specializes in financial services. I continue to learn new things everyday in and outside of work to truly master my craft of front end engineering.
